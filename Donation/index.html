Thank you for donating to the aid the people of Ukraine!

As many of you know, we have teamed up with Vova Khomenko, a Ukrainian who is living and working in Warsaw, Poland.

Since day 3 of the Russian invasion of Ukraine, Vova has been active in providing relief directly to those who are most in need.
As those who have already donated know, the priority is on transparency and the guarantee that your help reaches those individuals providing support on the front lines.

As always, we are gathering funds for medical supplies, winter clothes and boots, protective gear, tactical backpacks, and electronics such as powerbanks and communication equipment.

P.S. I have received requests from people wanting to donate larger sums of money.

As a special thank you, for every $500 that you donate I will give you a free one-hour online tai chi lesson! After donating, please contact me and we can schedule your session.
